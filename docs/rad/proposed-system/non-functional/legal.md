# 3.3.8 Legal

Legal requirements concern the obligations arising from handling the Traveler's personal data and from distributing the software.

**NFR-L.1 — Confinement of personal data.** All data entered by the Traveler shall remain on the device. The system shall transmit no personal data to any third party and shall collect neither analytics nor telemetry.

**NFR-L.2 — Non-recoverable credentials.** Credentials shall be stored so that they cannot be recovered by anyone holding the stored data, including the development team.

**NFR-L.3 — Protection of stored content.** Personal data and conversation content shall be unreadable to anyone inspecting the stored data without the key held by the operating system.

**NFR-L.4 — Third-party licensing.** Every third-party component incorporated into the system shall carry a licence compatible with the project's own licence.

## Deferred to future releases

Once accounts, transmission, and a service operator exist, the platform shall comply with the applicable data-protection regulation — including the right to obtain a machine-readable copy of one's data and the right to have it erased — shall present terms of service and a privacy policy for acceptance at registration, shall retain an auditable record of consent, and shall protect data in transit as well as at rest.
